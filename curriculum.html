About Curriculum

Our Coding Curriculum is a dynamic, engaging learning pathway that introduces young learners to the world of computer science. It blends flexible self-paced lessons with interactive, project-based activities, all supported by experienced instructors.

  • Self-Paced Learning: Students progress at a comfortable pace, supporting a low-pressure, adaptable experience.
  • Instructor Support: Our instructors are available to answer questions and keep learning on track.
  • Project-Based Activities: Every concept is reinforced through hands-on projectsâ€”game design, animations, and interactive storytelling.
  • Fundamental Concepts: Learners are introduced to loops, variables, sequencing, conditionals, and event-driven programming using intuitive block-coding tools such as Scratch.
  • Creativity & Problem-Solving: Projects nurture imagination, critical thinking, and troubleshooting skills.
  • Collaborative Community: Students share projects and offer peer feedback in a moderated, inclusive environment.

About Me

As an experienced educator, I have a deep-seated passion for inspiring young minds. My expertise spans a wide range of coding languages and platforms, from creating websites using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to developing games with Unity and C#. I have expertise in teaching Scratch, a platform that I've used to introduce children to the fundamentals of programming. A significant part of my teaching experience involves instructing coding in traditional classroom settings, allowing me to foster an engaging and interactive learning environment. Academically, I hold a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), equipping me with a strong theoretical foundation to guide my practical teaching approach. My mission is to ignite curiosity, encourage creativity, and build confidence in my students as they navigate the exciting world of coding.
