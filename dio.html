Online After School Program

Ages: 6-12

Instructor/Student:1 to 6

Details:

Our after school programs have no need for software installs. We teach the concepts of game design in scratch that involve the basic computer science fundamenntals. By the end of the program, students will know how create animations, utilize fundamental game concepts such as collison, and develop fascinating games using fundamental computer science concepts such as

  • loops
  • conditionals
  • variables

  • sequencing

Ranks:
